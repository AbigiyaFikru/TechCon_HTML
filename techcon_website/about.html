Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup for developers and entrepreneurs. Over the years, it has grown into an international event drawing thousands of attendees from across the globe. TechCon has hosted events in major cities like San Francisco, Berlin, and Tokyo.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lin

A pioneer in artificial intelligence and ethics, Dr. Lin delivered a landmark keynote in 2018 that explored the balance between automation and human-centered design.

Raj Patel

Raj, founder of GreenStack, spoke in 2020 about sustainable development in the tech industry and how innovation can coexist with environmental responsibility.

Samantha Osei

As a leader in blockchain technology, Samantha inspired attendees with her insights on decentralization and future financial systems.
